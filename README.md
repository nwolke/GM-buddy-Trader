# TTRPG Stock Trading Tool Concept

## 🎯 Original Idea
- **Core Concept**: A stock trading subsystem for TTRPGs.  
- **GM Setup**:  
  - Choose **genre focus** (fantasy, sci‑fi, superhero, etc.).  
  - Add **PCs** and **assets** they can trade.  
  - Start with **generic companies/corporations** (≈10), expandable by GM.  
- **Time Progression**:  
  - Controlled manually by GM, not automatic.  
  - Random **asset price changes** occur.  
  - Random **world events** presented to GM (accept/alter/reject).  
  - GM can manually adjust assets/events.  
- **AI Role**: Assist with generating random events.

---

## 🧠 Follow‑Up Thoughts

### 🎲 TTRPG Design Perspective
- **Agency vs. Control**: GM veto power is good, but balance with locked‑in events to avoid “rigged” feel.  
- **Genre Flavor**: Each genre should have **unique asset classes** and **event archetypes**.  
- **Narrative Hooks**: Tie market shifts to story beats (e.g., dragon destroys mine → ore prices spike).  
- **Player Engagement**: PCs should influence markets via **actions** (sabotage, lobbying, adventuring).

### 📈 Day Trader Perspective
- **Market Dynamics**: Add **trends, liquidity shocks, insider knowledge** for realism.  
- **Event Weighting**: Scale impact (minor scandal vs. war declaration).  
- **Feedback Loops**: Events ripple across systems (guild scandal → potion prices → adventurer performance).  
- **Risk & Speculation**: Options, leverage, manipulation mechanics for depth.

### 🤖 AI Event Generation
- **Event Creation**: AI spins genre‑specific, narrative‑rich events.  
- **Market Impact Modeling**: AI assigns plausible shifts based on severity/interconnections.  
- **GM Tools**: AI suggests multiple event variants for GM to pick/tweak.  
- **Rumors/News**: AI generates headlines—some true, some false—for speculation.

---

## ⚠️ Criticisms / Pitfalls
- **Complexity Overload**: Too realistic = disengagement. Keep accessible but deep.  
- **GM Burden**: Manual adjustments could slow pacing. AI should lighten load.  
- **Player Buy‑In**: Not all groups want trading sims—make it optional.  
- **Balance Risks**: Avoid exploitable “optimal strategies” that break immersion.

---

## 🚀 Elevation Ideas
- **Market News Feed**: AI‑generated headlines for immersion.  
- **Faction Influence**: PCs ally with guilds/corps/villains to sway markets.  
- **Event Decks**: Prebuilt genre decks remixable by AI.  
- **Visible Trends**: Charts/dashboards for speculation.  
- **Roleplay Integration**: Tie trading outcomes to adventuring rewards.

---

## 🔑 Summary
Your idea merges **EVE Online‑style economies** with **TTRPG narrative control**. Success hinges on:  
- **Alive, interconnected markets**  
- **Narrative integration**  
- **Balanced GM/player agency**  
- **AI‑assisted event richness**
